1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Duy Cao ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the use of the AI XCTestGenerator mobile application (the "App").

The App is a developer tool that helps generate XCTest files and test cases for Xcode projects and Swift source files. We take privacy seriously and aim to collect as little data as possible.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
